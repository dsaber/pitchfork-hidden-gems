Recommend Predict p4k's hidden gems

I created Pitchfork's Hidden Gems as my capstone project at Zipfian Academy. It combines a number of passions - music, data science, data products, and natural language processing - into a single platform for discovering new music.

The premise is simple: People are really good at explaining why they like things, but they're not so good at turning those feelings into a numeric rating. By developing an algorithm to score reviews based solely on content, I hope to turn up interesting albums that music lovers might have missed on the basis of a low review score (and that traditional recommender systems would have a hard time finding).

So... Click 'Recommend' to find new music and 'Predict' to objectively score your own longform music reviews. Enjoy!
